  • Real‐Time Visual Feedback Benchmark Creation:
    A Human‐and‐Metric‐in‐the‐loop workflow

    Anjana Arunkumar, Swaroop Mishra, Bhavdeep Sachdeva, Chitta Baral, Chris Bryan

    2023

    EACL

    This paper introduces VAIDA, a system designed to improve the quality of NLP benchmarks by guiding crowdworkers with real-time visual feedback and metric-based recommendations. The system significantly reduces artifacts in data samples, leading to better, more robust benchmarks. It achieved a 45.8% reduction in artifact levels and improved the diversity and robustness of models across tasks.

    NumGLUE: A Suite of Fundamental yet Challenging Mathematical Reasoning Tasks

    Swaroop Mishra, Arindam Mitra, Neeraj Varshney, Bhavdeep Singh Sachdeva, Peter Clark, Chitta Baral, Ashwin Kalyan

    2022

    ACL

    NumGLUE offers a benchmark that tests models' mathematical reasoning skills. It focuses on creating challenges that test fundamental math reasoning, revealing gaps in model understanding and pushing models to improve on this front.

    Generalized but not Robust? Understanding the Effects of Out‐of‐Domain Generalization Methods

    Tejas Gokhale, Swaroop Mishra, Man Luo, Bhavdeep Singh Sachdeva, Chitta Baral

    2022

    ACL

    This study investigates how generalization techniques can improve performance in NLP models, particularly in out-of-domain tasks. The findings emphasize the trade-off between generalization and robustness, urging improvements in both aspects.

    DQI: A Guide to Benchmark Evaluation

    Swaroop Mishra, Anjana Arunkumar, Bhavdeep Sachdeva, Chitta Baral

    2020

    ICML Workshop on Uncertainty and Robustness in Deep Learning

    DQI presents a framework to assess benchmark quality by identifying and correcting artifacts in datasets. It provides guidelines for better sample creation in NLP benchmarks, promoting more robust and reliable evaluations.
