Calls that actually help patients.

Before every call, the Care Gap Engine evaluates the patient against 69 clinical rules mapped to 10 guideline bodies (USPSTF, ADA, AHA, HEDIS, KDIGO, GOLD, ACIP, CMS, ACS, CDC).

Pre-Call Analysis

✓ Overdue screenings (mammogram, colonoscopy, eye exam)
✓ Lab monitoring (HbA1c, eGFR, lipids, BP)
✓ Medication adherence checks
✓ Behavioral assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
✓ Immunization status

Condition-Specific

Diabetes → blood sugar, foot checks. CHF → daily weight, fluid. COPD → inhaler technique. Not a generic script.
